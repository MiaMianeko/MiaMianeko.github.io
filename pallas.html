Pallas Entertainment.

Event Entertainment and Performances.

In this project, we need to implement a website on the topic of our choice. We choose to develop a website for performers to share their performances and sell tickets.

Framework

The goal of this project is to learn and use Html, Javascript, CSS, and PHP.

Challenge

This is the first time I worked as a team to develop a complete website. It is a challenge to organize our team schedule, separate tasks, and deliver the final product.

Project Process

In this project, we have different phases as the course goes further. In the first phase, we learn HTML and CSS. So we are brainstorming the idea of the project. And we finally decided the topic should be a website for performers. In the next phase, we learned JavaScript, so we implemented the front end: the homepage, about page, FAQ page. And the third phase, we know PHP, so we implement the backend database: user data, booking ticket system,

The idea

The idea of the project is to give a platform for people to show their performance. Our target user population is performers who want to share their concerts information and sell tickets, the audience interested in different performances and wish to buy their tickets. The platform gives a chance for them to share and purchase and collect feedback from the audience. The main goal of this project is to share the art with everyone.

The Final Product

I have implemented the home page's navbar, the FAQ page, and the reset password feature in this project.
