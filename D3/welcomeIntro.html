Species Extinction Interactive Slideshow

Welcome to our interactive slideshow on species extinction. This project aims to highlight the impact of human activities on biodiversity and the urgent need for conservation efforts.

Many people are becoming increasingly aware that their existence and actions might impact other species in profound ways. The loss of habitats, climate change, pollution, and overexploitation of resources are driving many species to the brink of extinction.

As awareness grows, efforts to mitigate these impacts are intensifying. From establishing protected areas to promoting sustainable practices, people worldwide are seeking ways to preserve the natural world and its inhabitants.

This narrative slideshow provides a comprehensive view of species extinction through the following lenses:

  • Red List of Countries: Explore the changes in the Red List Index over time, showcasing the status of biodiversity in different nations.
  • Species Diversity: Compare the number of known species with the number of endangered species in various countries.
  • Protected Areas: Examine the extent of terrestrial and marine protected areas and their role in conservation.

Navigate through the slides to understand the critical challenges facing our planet's biodiversity and discover the efforts being made to combat species extinction. Your journey through this slideshow will not only inform but also inspire action to protect our precious wildlife.
